# 'Feel like shit' Checklist

<SecondaryText>6 November, 2025</SecondaryText>

---

- **Slap on [Lucky Man by The Verve](https://youtu.be/MH6TJU0qWoY?si=DsIdqt8oQMRezl3U).**<br/>_Immediately. Full volume. Ideally in the car._
- **Pet a dog.**<br/>_Preferably a fluffy one, with a big personality._
- **Write down the ugliest thought in your head.**<br/>_Crumple it up. Burn it._
- **Ask your best friend how their day's going.**<br/>_The world is a mirror. It gives you what you give it._
- **Listen to a [Chris Williamson](https://www.youtube.com/@ChrisWillx) podcast.**<br/>_Either with [Naval Ravikant](https://youtu.be/KyfUysrNaco?si=Oj8AglpZZfk4Dr26) or [Alain de Botton](https://youtu.be/BQHo-z-pA8E?si=YSzTaTWvtkFBvkBj)._
- **Have a shower.**<br/>_Get out feeling fresh as f#%k and ready to GSD\*_
