I was watching a YouTube documentary called [The Ridiculous Engineering of Figma by Enrico Tartarotti](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5t8dh3DSdBk) earlier today - which is an incredible 30 minute demonstration on sensible interaction defaults by the way - and at [20:56](https://youtu.be/5t8dh3DSdBk?t=1256) in the video I got reminded about the most obnoxious UI trend of the post-covid world.

The classicly unclassic "AI UI". The one that made you immediately know you weren't going to get what you asked for. You know, the multicolour, glimouring, gradient cross-fading, sparkly emoji nonesense.
